TIMELINE

  • September 4, 1981: Born in Houston, Texas
  • 1997 - 2004: Destiny’s Child debuts its first single “No, No, No” and stays active, releasing six albums
  • December 10, 2002: Jay-Z and Beyonce release “03 Bonnie and Clyde” single, starting work and personal relationships
  • June 22, 2003: Dangerously in Love is released as Beyonce’s first solo album.
  • September 5, 2006: B’Day is released
  • April 4, 2008: Beyonce and Jay-Z are married in New York City in a private ceremony.
  • November 18, 2008: I Am . . . Sasha Fierce is released.
